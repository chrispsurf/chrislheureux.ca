About

My career didn’t start in a boardroom.

I started in the technical trenches solving problems, building systems, and understanding how things actually work. Over time, I moved into leading projects, then teams, then entire delivery organizations. What never changed was my focus on execution.

I lead complex enterprise initiatives where clarity matters and stakes are real. When projects stall, when priorities collide, or when confidence starts to slip, I bring structure back into the room.

My strength isn’t theory. It’s disciplined delivery.

I’ve led professional services teams, stabilized escalated programs, and overseen portfolios with dozens of concurrent engagements. I build governance that makes sense, reporting that executives can trust, and environments where teams can perform without chaos.

I’m comfortable at every level — from technical architecture discussions to executive steering committees — because I’ve lived both sides.

What drives me is simple: build systems that work, build teams that are accountable, and deliver outcomes that stand up to scrutiny.
